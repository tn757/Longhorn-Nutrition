Types of Cats

Cats come in many breeds, such as the Siamese, Persian, Maine Coon, and Sphynx. Each has unique characteristics and personalities.

Cat Care

To care for a cat, provide fresh water, nutritious food, regular vet visits, and lots of affection. Cats also need mental stimulation and scratching posts.

Fun Facts

Did you know cats sleep for 12–16 hours a day? They also have a special ability to land on their feet when they fall, called the “righting reflex.”
